RULES

1. Use arrow keys to move your claw. Check the target point on planes.
2. Hold space bar to get your claw to the planes.
3. Catch the items.
4. Have fun!

ITEMS

: Gain points. Items on the lower plane worth more points.
: Gain 3 seconds of extra time.
: Collect 7 of them to get huge bonus scores!

TIPS

* Claw doesn't move down if the target is out of plane
* Collecting 7 golds is harder than expected!
